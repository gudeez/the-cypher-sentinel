♦ Editor’s Column ♦

This week's headlines reveal a troubling convergence of convenience and risk as OpenAI integrates personal file storage into ChatGPT, potentially expanding the attack surface for cloud-based threats. Meanwhile, supply chain attacks continue to evolve, with adversaries weaponizing open-source tools like Trivy and abusing legitimate workflows such as VS Code auto-run tasks to deploy wipers and infostealers against specific geopolitical targets. Beyond technical exploits, the industry is grappling with the diminishing role of human oversight in AI-driven security operations and the rapid resurgence of phishing infrastructure disrupted just days prior. Ultimately, these stories underscore that while defenses advance, attackers are finding increasingly clever ways to exploit trust, automation, and legacy vulnerabilities before they can be patched.
